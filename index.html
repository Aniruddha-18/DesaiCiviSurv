Consulting Civil Engineer's & Surveyor's

A Civil Engineer designs, builds, and maintains infrastructure projects and systems in both the public and private sectors. These engineers ensure that buildings, roads, bridges, and water supply systems are safe and sustainable.

A Surveyor measures and maps the Earth's physical features. They use specialized tools to determine distances, angles, elevations, and boundaries, crucial for construction and land development.
