Civil engineering is a professional discipline that deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment, including works like roads, bridges, canals, dams, and buildings. Civil engineers ensure that infrastructure is safe, efficient, and sustainable, contributing to urban development and improved living conditions.

Surveying is the technique, profession, and science of determining the terrestrial or three-dimensional positions of points and the distances and angles between them. It plays a critical role in civil engineering, construction, and land development by providing accurate data for planning and execution of projects.
